# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want WhereMyTokens to be a useful, respectful project for people building local-first developer tools.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about risks and bugs.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Avoid sharing private logs, credentials, or personal data in public issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting secrets, access tokens, or unredacted local logs.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.
